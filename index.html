FAQ

Here are some common questions about our iPhone products:

How long is the warranty on your iPhone products?

We provide a standard one-year warranty on all our iPhone products. This warranty covers any manufacturing defects or malfunctions that may occur within the specified period.

Are your iPhone products unlocked or tied to a specific carrier?

Our iPhone products are unlocked, which means they can be used with any compatible carrier. You have the flexibility to choose and switch between carriers according to your preference and needs.

What is your return policy for iPhone purchases?

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy for all our iPhone products. If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you can return the product within 30 days of delivery for a refund or exchange.

Do your iPhone products come with original accessories?

Yes, all our iPhone products come with original accessories provided by the manufacturer. This includes the charging cable, power adapter, and earphones, ensuring you have everything you need to use your iPhone right out of the box.

How do I transfer my data from my old iPhone to a new one?

To transfer data from your old iPhone to a new one, you can use the built-in iCloud or iTunes backup and restore functionality. Alternatively, you can use Apple's Quick Start feature or the iOS migration assistant during the setup process to transfer your data wirelessly.
